FAQs

Will the Kia vehicle I have ordered come with the new Kia badge?
While our new Kia logo has officially launched, the changeover of badges across our product range will be a gradual process and commence with model year 2022 vehicles.
When will the new Kia logo badge arrive?
The new Kia logo badge will begin to arrive with Model Year 2022 vehicle orders. All Model Year 2022 Kia vehicles ordered after this point will come fitted with the new Kia logo as the badge.
Are KIA Parts and Accessories covered by warranty?
KIA Parts and Accessories installed at the time of a retail sale of a new motor vehicle are covered by the KIA New Vehicle Warranty applicable to your vehicle, depending on the vehicle’s use.
Will my warranty be affected if I have the vehicle modified?
The KIA New Vehicle Warranty will continue to apply to vehicles that may have been modified. However, KIA does not warrant the workmanship of, nor any part, component, assembly, equipment or accessory that has not been supplied by and approved by KIA for the specified purpose.
Is towing covered by the KIA New Vehicle Warranty?
If a component covered by the KIA New Vehicle Warranty fails as a result of poor workmanship or materials, rendering the vehicle immobile or undriveable, the cost of towing to the nearest authorised KIA dealer is generally covered under warranty.
Can I convert my vehicle to use LPG?
KIA does not manufacture LPG compatible engines for vehicles imported to Australia. Therefore, KIA advises that LPG should not be used in its range of petrol vehicles.
Where do I go to obtain KIA warranty and service?
For a full listing of Authorised KIA Dealers, visit our KIA Dealers section on our website - www.kia.com.au - or call KIA Customer Service on 131 KIA (131 542).
What items are not covered by the KIA New Vehicle Warranty?
Any damage caused when a non-genuine KIA part or accessory has been used. Any defects caused by repairs or replacement by other than an Authorised KIA Dealer. Any damage considered to be part of normal maintenance, such as cleaning and polishing.
Are tyres covered by the KIA New Vehicle Warranty?
The tyre manufacturer covers the original tyres fitted to the vehicle. Should you have any concerns regarding the tyres on your vehicle, contact your Authorised KIA Service Dealer for assistance.
Can I get my Kia Dealer to update the logo on my current Kia Vehicle?
Changing your previous Kia badge on your current vehicle is not possible due to the physical design and shape of the new Kia logo.
Why do some vehicles already have the Kia logo overseas?
The new Kia logo application is different amongst global regions due to the production month of 2022 Model Year vehicles varying globally.
What is the new Kia ethos?
The new Kia brand ethos is ‘Movement that inspires'. We believe movement inspires ideas. That is why we create the space for inspiration with our bold new designs and original inventive products, creating user ownership and experiences that are simple, intuitive and integrated.
